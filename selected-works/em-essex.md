# Em Essex

Selected works by Em Essex

Em Essex is one of the most famous music artists from Hamilton, Ontario. Em is the owner of the Halley Labs label, and the Lapfox Trax label. Em's works are an important source for youtube remixers since about 2009. The website is here: <https://msx.horse/>
